# Frequent Autosave
Have you ever <cr>lost</c> a <co>hard completion</c>? <cp>A new best</c> you'd been working on for <cf>weeks</c>? All just because the game <cr>didn't save</c> when you needed it to? With <cg>Frequent Autosave</c> these days are no more!

This mod makes it so your game <cg>saves</c> <cy>every time</c> you complete a level or get a new best. All <cd>without</c> causing even a minor lag spike!

## Who is this for?
- <cg>Everyone</c> who doesn't want to lose their progress from crashes
- <cp>Android users</c> who lose progress after leaving the game
- <cg>All players</c> on all platforms who don't want to randomly lose progress anymore

<cy>Note:</c> This mod only saves locally. It <cr>does not</c> Save to the Cloud.

## Important Recommendation
Geometry Dash is notorious for <cr>randomly wiping players' saves</c> even without any mods installed. It is therefore recommended to also install HJfod's <cg>Backups mod</c> to minimize data loss. (see below)

<mod:hjfod.backups>
